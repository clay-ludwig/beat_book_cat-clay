**Local Government & Politics:**

*   “Talbot County Council passed a resolution on June 26, 2024, to place a question on the 2024 ballot to amend the county’s charter to raise residents’ taxes for public safety by up to 1 cent per $100 of assessed value above the revenue cap for seven years beginning July 1, 2026.”
*   “The previous 1-cent resolution in 2020 was approved by voters and allowed for the addition of 26 public safety positions.”
*   “The new resolution extends the funding for an additional two years.”
*   “Commission President Norm Bell recommended voting on a resolution to clarify voting registration requirements March 13, 2025, but the commission tabled it in a two-to-one vote.”
*   “Commissioner Katrina Greer expressed concerns that rushing into the vote could delay its effectiveness for the town’s upcoming election in June.”
*   “Commissioner Tom Costigan voted against tabling the resolution, but suggested writing rules for the Oxford Election Board instead.”
*   “The Easton Town Council unanimously approved a permit for the 2025 Delmarva Pride Festival on December 4, 2024.”
*   “The festival is set for June 14, 2025, and expected attendance is around 4,000.”
*   “Council President Frank Gunsallus and four other council members voted for approval, while Ward 3 Councilmember David Montgomery was absent from the meeting.”
