**Easton High Turf Field Project Uncertain:**

*   “The prospects of an artificial turf field at Easton High School are uncertain as the school year approaches.”
*   “The project was initially approved in December 2023 after Elgin Hawley, a Make-A-Wish wish recipient who suffered from an aneurysm on the field in 2021, requested it.”
*   “Talbot County Public Schools received only $1.5 million for capital projects this fiscal year, short of the estimated $1.8 million needed.”
*   “The Board of Education is considering alternative options, including a high-quality grass field, and has not made an official decision allocating funds to the project yet.”

**Overdose Arrest:**

*   “A Henderson man, Justin A. Bodman, was arrested on September 28, 2023, for manslaughter and narcotic distribution in connection to an overdose death in Easton that occurred on September 5, 2022.”

**Tunis Mills Bridge Replacement:**

*   “The Talbot County Council agreed to move forward with replacing the Leeds Creeks Bridge in Tunis Mills with a wooden deck and timber wearing surface, as proposed by County Engineer Ray Clarke, after months of resident input.”
*   “The project costs around $6.5 million, with $1.5 million coming from the county and the rest federally funded.”
*   “Advocates for a 100% wooden replacement, including Susan McDermott and John Tabori, expressed concerns but welcomed the changes to the proposal.”
*   “The council unanimously approved the project as presented, with Vice President Pete Lesher thanking Clarke's team for their efforts and expressing hope for its successful completion.”
