**Talbot County Senior Care Funding Cuts:**

*   “Senior care experts warn that state cuts could impact Talbot County seniors in fiscal year 2026 and potentially as early as this year's final quarter.”
*   “Initial proposals putting $2.5 million in funding at risk.”
*   “Carrie Ottey, director of senior services for the Talbot County Health Department, estimates a potential loss of $24,000 in funding if cuts are implemented.”
*   “Council members, including Pete Lesher and Lynn Mielke, express concern over the impact on senior care programs in the county, where 30% of residents are aged 65 and older.”
*   “The Maryland Department of Aging is facing an over $3 billion deficit, with state legislators pushing for a reduction in total cuts from $2.5 million to around $900,000.”
